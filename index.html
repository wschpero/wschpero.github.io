I am a Ph.D. candidate studying health policy and economics at Yale University.

I am also affiliated with the National Bureau of Economic Research, where I am currently a Pre-Doctoral Fellow in Aging and Health Economics.

In July 2019, I will join the Department of Healthcare Policy and Research at Weill Cornell Medical College as an assistant professor in the Division of Health Policy and Economics.

My research is focused on evaluating — and optimizing — the design of the U.S. health care safety net, with the ultimate goal of informing the evolution of relevant federal and state policy, as well as the work of health care providers and organizations that serve vulnerable and high-need, high-cost patient populations.
